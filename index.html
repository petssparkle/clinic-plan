·Why Clinics Buy This

A specific, painful problem aesthetic, dental, and beauty clinics in Egypt and the Gulf (and later the US) already pay people to solve badly. We solve it 24/7 for less than the cost of a part-time receptionist.

The pain

  • 100–500 DMs/week on Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger
  • 80% are the same 5 questions: price, hours, availability, location, "send photos"
  • Receptionists spend most of the day re-typing the same answers
  • Inquiries arriving after-hours / weekends go cold
  • Ad-driven leads (Meta/Google) wait hours for a reply — they book elsewhere

What we replace

  • Hiring a 2nd receptionist (~$800–1,500/mo in Egypt + the Gulf)
  • Cobbled-together Manychat / Zapier / Calendly stacks
  • Generic chatbots that hallucinate or sound robotic
  • Lost ad spend on leads that never get a reply
  • Missed appointment bookings at night & on weekends

What changes for the clinic

  • Every patient message gets an instant, on-brand reply
  • Ad-driven DMs auto-respond with the matching offer + booking link
  • Appointments book into the calendar without staff lifting a finger
  • 24/7 coverage, even peak inquiry hours in Egypt + Gulf (evenings & weekends)
  • Replies come from the clinic's own fixed list — AI doesn't chat with patients, just picks the matching reply and sends it. No surprises, no hallucinations.

01The Product & Pricing

Belal ships V1 by Aug 1 using Claude Code. The product replaces 60–80% of a clinic receptionist's workload by handling DMs, answering pricing questions, and booking appointments automatically.

Ship by Aug 1

  • Unified Inbox — Instagram DM + Facebook Messenger + WhatsApp + Google Business Messages in one dashboard
  • Fixed-Reply Library (EN + AR) — the clinic writes its own list of replies for every common scenario (pricing, hours, services, "saw your ad" follow-ups, post-booking confirmations). AI's only job is to match the inbound message to the right reply and send it. Not a chatbot conversation. No AI-generated text. Owner controls every word that goes out.
  • Ad-Reply Automation — when a patient DMs after seeing a Meta or Google ad, AI detects the ad source and fires the clinic's pre-set reply with the offer, price, and booking link
  • Calendar Booking — AI checks availability and books appointments directly into Google Calendar / Outlook / the clinic's scheduler
  • Lead Capture — every inquiry becomes a contact record · auto-tagged by service, source, urgency
  • Human Handoff — when intent is unclear or the patient asks for a human, one-click hand-off to staff
  • Basic Analytics — inquiries answered, bookings made, response time, channel volume

Not in V1 — cut on purpose

  • Voice / phone-call AI agent (text only in V1)
  • Custom AI fine-tuning per clinic
  • Native mobile app
  • EHR / patient-record integration
  • Billing & insurance processing
  • Multi-clinic team management
  • Marketing campaign automation

Top 3 risks

1 — Channel API approval timelines. Meta WhatsApp Business + Instagram Graph API onboarding can take 2–4 weeks each (sometimes longer for new businesses). Mitigation: apply for Meta Business + WhatsApp Business in May, not July. Have manual handoff ready as a fallback if any channel is still pending approval at launch.

2 — Clinic-owner resistance + receptionist pushback. Owners worry the AI will go off-script or that staff will feel replaced. Mitigation: position as "pre-approved replies your clinic writes, AI just sends them" — owner stays in full control. Frame for staff as "kills the boring 80% so you focus on real bookings." Every clinic gets a 60-min onboarding to set the tone.

3 — Patient data privacy (GDPR · UAE PDPL · KSA PDPL). Patient messages contain personal + sometimes health-adjacent info. Mitigation: data residency in EU or UAE where required, end-to-end encryption at rest, no sharing across clinics, clear DPA in the ToS. Avoid US HIPAA territory in Phase 1 by sticking to non-medical aesthetic clinics — we revisit for medical/dental in Phase 2.
